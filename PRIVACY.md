# Privacy Policy — Moto Vitals

_Last updated: 19 July 2026_

Moto Vitals ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** your email address, used to sign in and identify your garage.
- **Vehicle data:** details you add about your motorcycle, including make, model, odometer readings, purchase date, and maintenance history.
- **Usage data:** basic app interaction data used to fix bugs and improve features.

## How we use your information

- To operate your garage dashboard and keep your vehicle and maintenance data in sync across sessions.
- To send you maintenance reminders and service alerts you've asked for within the app.
- To improve Moto Vitals based on how the app is actually used.

## Affiliate and marketplace links

Moto Vitals may display links to third-party retailers (such as Lazada, Shopee, or AliExpress) for motorcycle parts. If you purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We do not share your personal vehicle data with these retailers — clicking a link simply opens their site or app.

## Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Your choices

You can edit or delete your vehicle data at any time from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us using the details below.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@motovitals.app.
