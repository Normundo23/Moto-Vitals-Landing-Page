About

Built by riders who were tired of guessing.

Moto Vitals started as a simple frustration: motorcycle maintenance intervals live in a paper manual, a mechanic's memory, or nowhere at all. We wanted a dashboard that behaves like the one on the bike itself — calm when everything's fine, and impossible to ignore when it isn't.

We're currently testing with riders across the Philippines, starting with popular commuter bikes like the Honda Winner X, before expanding to more makes and models.
